About

Mrs. Koshy motivates children to improve their grades and test scores, and to acquire a passion for learning that will last them a lifetime. With more than 25 years of dedicated teaching experience, Mrs. Koshy has been successful in providing supportive assistance to children and has been instrumental in bolstering and improving their skills, self-esteem and confidence to succeed in school.

Mrs. Koshy specializes in tutoring Reading, Writing, Mathematics and Coaching for Standardized Achievement Tests. Through her tutoring, your child will be able to learn and practice subject areas as well as develop the skills of problem-solving, flexibility, and creativity. Mrs. Koshy also promotes positive study habits which will empower them to be the best they can be.

Mrs. Achamma Koshy has over 25 years of teaching experience at the Hudson Montessori School (HMS), one of the most prestigious Montessori schools in the country. She also holds a B.S. degree in Physics with a minor in Mathematics, and a Diploma from the Association Montessori Internationale (AMI).
